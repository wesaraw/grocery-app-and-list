1. Lemon Herb Chicken
* Marinade:

   * 1 tbsp olive oil

   * 1 tsp lemon juice

   * 1/2 tsp dried thyme

   * 1/2 tsp garlic powder

   * Pinch salt and pepper

      * Air Fryer: 375°F for 18 min, flip at 9 min
Sides: Buttered baby potatoes + garlic green beans
      * Ingredients:

         1. Baby potatoes (~1 cup per meal)

         2. Green beans (~1 cup)

         3. 1 tbsp butter

         4. 1 clove garlic, minced

         5. Salt & pepper

            * Instructions:

               1. Boil potatoes until fork-tender (10–15 min), then sauté in butter with salt.

               2. In a pan, sauté garlic in a little oil, add green beans, cook until tender but still crisp.
